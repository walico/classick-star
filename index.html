Interior & Exterior Painting

We deliver professional interior and exterior painting for walls, trim, and facades. Whether you want a fresh look or lasting protection, we use quality materials and careful prep so the job looks great and holds up.

Flooring Installation & Repair

Tile, vinyl, laminate, and hardwood—we install and repair all types of flooring. Our team ensures a level subfloor, clean cuts, and a finish that stands up to daily use.

Kitchen Installation & Renovation

At Classic Star, we design and build kitchens tailored to your style and needs. Whether you want a sleek modern space or a classic look, we deliver quality craftsmanship, functionality, and attention to detail to create the heart of your home.

Bathroom Remodeling

From fixture updates to full remodels, we handle bathroom projects with care. We work with tile, plumbing, and finishes so your bathroom is functional, watertight, and easy to maintain.

Drywall Installation & Repair

We hang, patch, and finish drywall for new construction and repairs. Smooth seams and a clean finish mean your walls are ready for paint or wallpaper.

Plumbing & Electrical

We handle minor plumbing and electrical work as part of maintenance and renovations—fixes, fixture installs, and small upgrades. For larger or permitted work, we’ll point you in the right direction.

General Repairs & Handyman

From small fixes to larger projects, we tackle repairs and handyman work with the same quality and reliability. One call handles a long to‑do list so you can enjoy your home without the hassle.

Office & Commercial Property Maintenance

We keep office and commercial properties in good shape with routine maintenance, repairs, and small improvements. Dependable service so your business space stays safe and professional.

Preventive Maintenance Programs

Scheduled inspections and upkeep help avoid costly surprises. We tailor a preventive plan to your property so systems and finishes last longer and small issues are caught early.

Property Make-Ready Services

We get properties ready for new tenants or sale—repairs, paint, cleaning, and small upgrades so every unit looks and functions at its best. Turnkey service so you can list or lease with confidence.

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Classic Star Home Maintenance.

What services does Classic Star offer?

We offer interior and exterior painting; flooring installation and repair (tile, vinyl, laminate, hardwood); kitchen installation and renovation; bathroom remodeling; drywall installation and repair; minor plumbing and electrical; general home repairs and handyman services; office and commercial property maintenance; preventive maintenance programs; and property make-ready services.

How do I schedule a visit or get a quote?

You can call us at (574) 544-9743 or use the contact form on this site. We’ll discuss your needs and set up a time that works for you. Quotes are provided after we understand the scope of the job.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Mishawaka, Indiana, and serve the surrounding area. Contact us with your address and we’ll let you know if we can come to your location.

Do you offer emergency or same-day service?

We do our best to accommodate urgent requests when possible. Call us to describe the issue and we’ll let you know our soonest availability. For true emergencies (e.g. water or safety issues), we prioritize those when scheduling.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Classic Star Home Maintenance LLC is a licensed, insured company. We take responsibility for our work and protect your property. You can ask for proof of insurance when we provide your quote.

What are your hours?

Our office hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on weekends. You can leave a message or send an email outside these hours and we’ll get back to you as soon as we can.
